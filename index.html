My Projects

Housing Rent Prediction

In the heart of the digital wilderness, this project commences with the promise of deciphering the cryptic world of real estate pricing.

The Tale goes as:

  • The Data Chronicles:The project's journey commences with a treasure chest of housing data, rich with property features, locations, and, most importantly, their prices.
  • The Algorithmic Odyssey: The journey takes us through the intricate woods of regression models, decision trees, and ensemble techniques.
  • The Test of Fortitude:The models are pit against rigorous trials, splitting the data into training and testing sets. Metrics like RMSE and MAE become the yardsticks by which success is measured.
  • The Grand Finale:A web application or API is a gateway to share the magic with the world.

Movie Recommendation System

Welcome to the world of Movie Magic! I've embarked on a thrilling journey to create a Movie Recommendation System that transforms movie-watching into an interactive adventure. In this narrative, you become the protagonist, and your preferences shape the story.

The Tale Unfolds:
Once upon a time, in the land of cinematic wonders, a recommendation system emerged. Its mission was to provide movie enthusiasts with the ultimate viewing experience. But this wasn't just any system; it was designed to understand you, the viewer.

  • Discover Your Movie Persona: You share your preferences and viewing history, and the system becomes your guide, helping you discover your unique movie persona.
  • The Content Quest: Utilizing content filtering, the system collaborates with fellow movie lovers to unearth hidden gems and popular classics alike.
  • The Data Chronicles: Our journey involves a treasure trove of movie data, which we meticulously preprocess to create a seamless viewing experience.
  • The Magic Algorithms: Along the way, we encounter recommendation algorithms that analyze your choices, including user-based and item-based filtering
  • Judgment Day: As the adventure unfolds, we face a critical juncture - the evaluation stage, where the system's performance is assessed to ensure your movie journey is nothing short of magical.
